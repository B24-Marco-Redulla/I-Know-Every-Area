WHAT IS IKEA?

IKEA is a Swedish multinational conglomerate based in the Netherlands that designs and sells ready-to-use furniture, kitchen appliances, decoration, and many more things.

I Know Every Area was made to help customers find their way through the IKEA in the Mall of Asia in the fastest way possible, as it is not uncommon for customers to get lost in the maze-like floors and end up wasting a lot of time trying to get from one point to another.
